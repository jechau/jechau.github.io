About

Piggy Pin

My name is Jenny Chau and I am a twenty-something designerd from the outskirts of Philly, PA.

I enjoy designing and illustrating bespoke goodies for cool people.

I operate an Etsy shop called Love Lychee where I sell enamel pins and printables. The name derives from the legend of Yang Guifei and her love for lychee (as you can see from the current logo for my shop, she has since reincarnated into a loveable corgi).

2020. Jenny Chau.
